RankMyFans’ Privacy Policy

RankMyFans is an app providing registered users of Instagram to download, save, re-post photos; create and share your Instagram’s photo albums. RankMyFans also allows you to locate other Instagram’s users. (the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy describes how and when RankMyFans collects, uses and shares your information when you use our Services. RankMyFans receives your information through our APIs, applications and from our partners and other third parties. For example, you send us information when you use RankMyFans app, or access Instagram from an application such as RankMyFans Apps for iOS, or Android. When using any of our Services you consent to the collection, transfer, manipulation, storage, disclosure and other uses of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Irrespective of which country you reside in or supply information from, you authorize RankMyFans to use your information in any country where RankMyFans operates.

RankMyFans may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and traffic routing on our Services, and to customize and improve our Services. Most applications automatically accept cookies.

Google Analytics: We will also install Google Analytics in the apps, devices, or mobile apps to collect your behaviors in using our Services. We will also recommend some other services that may be suitable or appeal to you based on our analysis.
